A data-based approach to sustainable fashion.

Sustainability has to include lifespan & transparency. We design fewer, better garments; document the chain; and create for care, repair, and repeat wear. "What do this even mean?" Well...

01 multimodal

Designed to wear multiple ways

Convertible closures, modular construction, reversible details. So one piece stays relevant across settings.

02 trace-first

Who made it. Where. How.

We share with you our artisans' stories & document component-level disclosure with traceable paths: origin → construction → finish → distribution. We publish what’s confirmed and what’s in progress.

03 created to last

Here for a good time, and a long time

APARERI focuses on construction-first patterning, stress-point reinforcement, and repair-friendly choices. Durability is our core sustainability metric.

FAQ

A few answers while Hera Collection is in the works.

When is the first drop?

We're currently post-prototyping and will drop as soon as we set the quality of everything in place. Join the waitlist for first access updates.

Will you publish sourcing details even if they’re imperfect?

Yes. The goal is honest documentation: what we know, what we’re improving, and what’s next.

What makes the pieces “created to last”?

Material performance, construction choices, care guidance, and a repair-forward design mindset.

Do you track visitors?

No trackers. We only use the information you share to contact you about important updates.
