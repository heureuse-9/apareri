About APARERI

Luxury engineered for longevity—designed to be worn, repeated, repaired, and kept.

APARERI is a sustainable luxury concept focused on one core idea: if a piece is designed to last and designed to be worn often, it becomes both more luxurious and more responsible.

What you can expect

  • Modular silhouettes with repeatable styling logic
  • Trace-first sourcing and documentation that improves over time
  • Care + repair guidance built into the product story

Claims like “plastic-free” and “colorfast” are finalized only once materials pass testing. Until then, we label them as goals or in-progress.
